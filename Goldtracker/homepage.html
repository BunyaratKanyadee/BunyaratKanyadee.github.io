How often is the gold price updated?

The gold price updates in real time based on market data.

What determines the price of gold?

The price of gold is determined by global financial markets, supply and demand, inflation, and economic conditions.

Why does the gold price change frequently?

Gold prices change frequently because they react to market conditions, currency values, interest rates, and geopolitical events.

Is the gold price the same worldwide?

The base gold price is generally the same worldwide, but the final price may differ depending on currency exchange rates and local market conditions.

What unit is used for gold pricing?

Gold is commonly priced per troy ounce in international markets, but this website allows conversion to grams.

Can I use this website on mobile devices?

Yes, the website is designed to work on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Does this website allow buying or selling gold?

No, this platform only provides gold price information and calculation tools.

How accurate is the gold price on this website?

The gold price comes from financial data sources and is updated frequently, but it may slightly differ from local dealers.

What currencies will be supported?
